Coastal Karnataka

Discover the pristine beaches, ancient temples, and vibrant culture of Karnataka's stunning coastline

Where Azure Waters Meet Golden Sands

Karnataka's 320-kilometer coastline along the Arabian Sea is a treasure trove of natural beauty, spiritual heritage, and cultural richness. From the pilgrimage town of Udupi to the beach paradise of Gokarna, each destination offers a unique blend of serenity and adventure.

Coastal Gems of Karnataka

Gokarna

Known as the "Varanasi of the South," Gokarna seamlessly blends spiritual significance with natural beauty. This sacred town offers pristine beaches, ancient temples, and a laid-back atmosphere that attracts both pilgrims and beach lovers.

Key Attractions:

Om Beach Mahabaleshwar Temple Kudle Beach Half Moon Beach

Udupi

The temple town of Udupi is famous for its Krishna temple, delicious South Indian cuisine, and rich cultural heritage. Experience the divine atmosphere, savor authentic Udupi delicacies, and explore the nearby pristine beaches.

Key Attractions:

Sri Krishna Temple Malpe Beach St. Mary's Island Udupi Cuisine

Murudeshwar

Home to the world's second-tallest Shiva statue, Murudeshwar offers a perfect blend of spirituality and scenic beauty. The towering statue overlooks the Arabian Sea, creating a breathtaking vista that leaves visitors spellbound.

Key Attractions:

Shiva Statue Murudeshwar Temple Beach Activities Water Sports

游 Conservation Legacy of Karnataka 游

Karnataka stands as a beacon of wildlife conservation in India, home to over 20% of the country's tiger population and numerous endemic species found nowhere else on Earth. From the misty forests of the Western Ghats to the dry deciduous woodlands of the Deccan Plateau, Karnataka's protected areas represent one of the most successful conservation stories in Asia. The state's commitment to preserving its natural heritage ensures that future generations will witness the same magnificent wildlife that has roamed these lands for millennia, maintaining the delicate balance between human progress and nature's sovereignty.

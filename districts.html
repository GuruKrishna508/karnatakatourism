Discover Karnataka

Explore the Rich Heritage and Diverse Beauty of Karnataka's Districts

Bengaluru

Area: 2,190 km²
Taluks:
Bengaluru North Bengaluru South Anekal Devanahalli
Speciality: Silicon Valley of India, IT Hub, Garden City with pleasant climate and cosmopolitan culture
Special Cuisine: Masala Dosa, Bisi Bele Bath, Mysore Pak, Filter Coffee, Rava Idli
Must Visit Places:
• Lalbagh Botanical Garden - Historic garden with rare plants and glasshouse
• Bangalore Palace - Tudor-style palace with beautiful architecture

Mysuru

Area: 6,854 km²
Taluks:
Mysuru Nanjangud T. Narasipura Hunsur Periyapatna Piriyapatna K.R. Nagar
Speciality: City of Palaces, Yoga Capital, Sandalwood City, Rich cultural heritage and royal history
Special Cuisine: Mysore Pak, Mysore Masala Dosa, Obbattu, Rasam, Puliyogare
Must Visit Places:
• Mysore Palace - Magnificent Indo-Saracenic palace with intricate artwork
• Chamundi Hills - Sacred hill with Chamundeshwari Temple and panoramic views

Dakshina Kannada (Mangaluru)

Area: 4,559 km²
Taluks:
Mangaluru Bantwal Puttur Sullia Belthangady
Speciality: Coastal beauty, Major port city, Educational hub, Pristine beaches and backwaters
Special Cuisine: Mangalorean Fish Curry, Neer Dosa, Kori Rotti, Gassi, Golibaje
Must Visit Places:
• Panambur Beach - Popular beach with water sports and sunset views
• St. Aloysius Chapel - Historic chapel with beautiful paintings and architecture

Udupi

Area: 3,575 km²
Taluks:
Udupi Kundapur Karkala
Speciality: Temple town, Birthplace of Udupi cuisine, Scenic coastal region with pristine beaches
Special Cuisine: Udupi Sambar, Masala Dosa, Rasam, Payasam, Gulla Bajji
Must Visit Places:
• Sri Krishna Temple - Ancient temple famous for its unique worship style
• Malpe Beach - Beautiful beach with water sports and St. Mary's Island nearby

Vijayanagara (Hampi)

Area: 6,918 km²
Taluks:
Hosapete Hagaribommanahalli Kottur Kushtagi
Speciality: UNESCO World Heritage Site, Ancient Vijayanagara Empire ruins, Historical architecture
Special Cuisine: Jolada Rotti, Shenga Chutney, Yennegai, Mutton Curry, Holige
Must Visit Places:
• Hampi Ruins - UNESCO World Heritage Site with magnificent temple complexes
• Vittala Temple - Famous for its stone chariot and musical pillars

Dharwad

Area: 4,263 km²
Taluks:
Dharwad Hubli Navalgund Kundgol Kalghatgi
Speciality: Twin cities, Educational hub, Cultural center, Cotton and textile industry
Special Cuisine: Dharwad Peda, Karadant, Jolada Rotti, Enne Gai, Benne Dose
Must Visit Places:
• Chandramouleshwara Temple - Ancient temple with intricate Chalukyan architecture
• Unkal Lake - Scenic lake perfect for boating and evening walks

Belagavi (Belgaum)

Area: 13,415 km²
Taluks:
Belagavi Gokak Khanapur Raibag Savadatti Parasgad
Speciality: Border district, Historical significance, Military cantonment, Jaggery production
Special Cuisine: Kunda, Mandige, Jolada Rotti, Mutton Curry, Belgaum Burfi
Must Visit Places:
• Gokak Falls - Spectacular waterfall known as the 'Niagara of India'
• Belgaum Fort - Historic fort built by Jaya Raya with Masjid inside

Shivamogga (Shimoga)

Area: 8,495 km²
Taluks:
Shivamogga Bhadravati Sagar Sorab Hosanagar Thirthahalli Shikaripura
Speciality: Gateway to Malnad, Waterfalls, Dense forests, Areca nut and spice cultivation
Special Cuisine: Akki Rotti, Neer Dosa, Kotte Kadubu, Coconut-based curries, Payasam
Must Visit Places:
• Jog Falls - India's second-highest waterfall with four distinct cascades
• Keladi Rameshwara Temple - Historic temple with exquisite Hoysala architecture

Chikkamagaluru

Area: 7,201 km²
Taluks:
Chikkamagaluru Kadur Mudigere Kalasa Koppa Narasimharajapura Sringeri
Speciality: Coffee Land of Karnataka, Hill station, Birthplace of Indian coffee, Scenic Western Ghats
Special Cuisine: Pandi Curry, Neer Dosa, Kadubu, Filter Coffee, Malnad specialties
Must Visit Places:
• Mullayanagiri Peak - Highest peak in Karnataka with breathtaking views
• Hebbe Falls - Spectacular waterfall surrounded by coffee plantations

Hassan

Area: 6,826 km²
Taluks:
Hassan Arsikere Belur Channarayapatna Holenarsipura Manjarabad Sakleshpur Alur
Speciality: Hoysala architecture, Historical temples, Agricultural hub, Arecanut and coffee cultivation
Special Cuisine: Mysore Pak, Obbattu, Bisi Bele Bath, Ragi Mudde, Ghee Roast
Must Visit Places:
• Belur Chennakeshava Temple - Masterpiece of Hoysala architecture with intricate carvings
• Halebidu Hoysaleswara Temple - UNESCO World Heritage Site with exquisite sculptures
